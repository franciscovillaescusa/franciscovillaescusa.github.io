The simulations and halo catalogues can be downloaded directly from the web browser or from the terminal using commands as wget or curl. The transfer can take place at a very slow rate. For a faster transfer create an account in globus and look for Simons Foundation Data Server in the first endpoint. Use fvillaescusa as ask us for the password.
