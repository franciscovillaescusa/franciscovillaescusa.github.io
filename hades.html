Welcome to the HADES simulations!

The HADES simulations are a set of more than 1000 publicly available N-body and hydrodynamic simulations with massless and massive neutrinos.

The purpose of the simulations is to be a tool to understand the impact of neutrino masses on cosmological observables.

The HADES simulations is the result of many years of development and research by the team members. The about page provides a detailed description of the simulations.

The simulations are stored in a server of the Center for Computational Astrophysics in New York, and can be downloaded from data.
